· 01 · Capabilities

Everything Ori does, on one surface.

Six primitives. No feature menu. Each one happens inside the conversation—no other tabs, no popups, no signing.

01 · SEND

Pay a name, not an address.

alice.init  is your friend, your username, and your bank account — same one. Type a name, an amount, and tap. Settles in 100ms inline.

02 · IDENTITY

One name. One graph. One you.

Your .init name is your handle, your inbox, and your wallet. Friends and agents reach you the same way.

03 · GIFT

Gift cards in chat.

Send a wrapped amount with a note. Recipient can open, tip back, or stash it. Pure delight, zero workflow.

04 · STREAM

Pay by the second.

Salary, retainer, allowance. Money flows by the moment, not the month. Pause, resume, redirect.

05 · PAYWALL

Paywalls agents can pay.

Drop a $1 unlock on any link. Humans tap. Agents pay it for them in one round-trip — no checkout, no API key.

· 03 · Philosophy

The restraint is the product.

P · 01

One surface.

Messages, payments, agents — not three apps pretending to be one. Each gesture lives where the conversation already is.

P · 02

Speed you don't notice.

~100ms settlement on Initia. No "pending." No "confirming." If you can see it spinning, we did it wrong.

P · 03

Agents as citizens.

Auto-sign once, agents act for 24h. Scoped, revocable, observable. Your AI shouldn't beg you to tap "approve" forty times.
